About Me

I am comfortable with abstract thinking, which helps me color the walls of the future with the texture of quality. I have a real knack for understanding what makes people tick and I'm interested in making and improving technology and software to prevent them from going boom, so to speak.

I have led many student projects using Agile methodologies and feel that with multiple experiences in management, teaching and training, I would operate well in a leadership position. I am also open to diversifying a bit to help in anyway I can. I have dabbled in video game design and innovation with projects focusing on software and board game creation and iteration to develop fun and engaging solutions and playables.

Much of my interest, work and research deals with the ways people communicate cooperate and navigate through information and the psychology and motives behind how it al goes down. I would love to eventually end up working to provide technical solutions to aid in healthcare delivery, methods and education.

I am dedicated to spreading my interests far and wide so as to soak up any and all information I can. I act and sing in my spare time and like to keep a well rounded set of interests and hobbies. Whether it's paintball, shooting handguns at the range, going out to tee off or just take a hike, I am always ready to take on a new challenge or experience. I am an open book, so if you're interested, message me.
